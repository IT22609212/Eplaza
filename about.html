E-Plaza – Sri Lanka’s biggest fashion chain offers a wide range of clothes and accessories for Men, Women and Kids. The retail store houses a range of Homeware and Lifestyle products to compliment a comprehensive family shopping experience for all our customers. Our showrooms are located in strategic cities across the Island, in addition to our head office which is located in Kandy.



E-Plaza- has been growing from strength to strength over the last 29 years with a team of 1800 people serving millions of customers every year. The retail chain takes pride in its Sri Lankan roots but offers a world-class retail experience that drives customer loyalty while simultaneously setting new benchmarks in customer-centricity to take the art of retailing to a new level.
